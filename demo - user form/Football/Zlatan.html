Zlatan Childhood

Zlatan Ibrahimovic was born on October 3, 1981, in Malmo, Sweden. The son of a Bosnian father and a Croatian mother, Ibrahimovic received his first pair of football boots at age five, and by age eight, he was playing for the youth team of FBK Balkan. He scored 8 goals in a match for his local club at the age of 8, and was later discovered by scouts from Malmo FF, one of Sweden's leading clubs.

He received a black belt in taekwondo at the age of 17, having practiced the sport for a decade. He has said that if he wasn't a footballer, he would have been a martial artist instead.

He grew up as a fan of the Brazilian striker Ronaldo, and his style of play has been compared to that of the Brazilian star.
