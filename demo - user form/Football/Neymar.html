Neymar Childhood

Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior was born on February 5, 1992, in Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo, Brazil. The son of a former professional soccer player, Neymar followed in his father's footsteps by playing street games and futsal, an indoor version of the game. He joined the Portuguesa Santista youth club in 1999, and within a few years was one of the most highly regarded young talents in the country.

He was born to Neymar Santos Sr. and Nadine da Silva. He inherited his name from his father, who is a former footballer and became his son's advisor as Neymar's talents began to grow. Neymar comments on his father's role: "My father has been by my side since I was little. He takes care of things, my finances and my family."

He has a younger sister named Rafaella Beckran. He also has an older sister named Leticia. He began dating Brazilian actress Bruna Marquezine in 2013 but the couple split up in 2014. He then began dating model Gabriella Lenzi, but the couple split up in 2015. He then began dating actress Chloe Grace Moretz in 2016.
